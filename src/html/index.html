About Me

Well if the url and the title hasn't already given it away my name is Andy Blackledge. I'm a front end web developer from Bolton, working at Building Block in Manchester, I've been there since March 2012 where I've had the good fortune to work with some superb developers.

I started off working on the support desk and progressed to the Front End team towards the end of 2013. I love what I do and because of the company that Building Blocks is I've been able to work on some pretty huge projects for multi-million dollar international clients, often taking designs from third party design agencies but sometimes being lucky enough to work with our own internal design agency.

I feel very lucky to be able to make a living in this industry, before getting into web development I worked in a number of jobs that really weren't for me so even the worst days now are still a hell of a lot better than the best days in my previous jobs.
